Mission

To recruit in the Akron/Canton, OH community, recent local high school graduates who have the aptitude, flair, and talent for IT services and provide them with training in technical, social and work culture so that in the short term they have the skills to meet customer expectations in a job. In the longer term, our goal is to train them in IT services so as to get customers to outsource work to us.

Vision

To build an IT services consulting firm that is respected by clients for the quality and service of its deliverable at a competitive price.

Values

Our clients’ interests will always come first – we firmly believe that if we serve our customers well, our own success will follow. We pride ourselves on our people and our reputation – we will dedicate ourselves to fully develop our people and establish among them a strong work ethic and commitment to excellence. Our reputation is critical in our industry and if it is ever diminished it is the most difficult to restore. We take pride in the professional quality of our work. We will always seek to under promise and over deliver. While profit making is essential to any business, for us it will never be at the expense of a customer or an employee.

Our Name, Our Story…

Mani Subramanian founded Fortisure Ventures LLC (the managing company of FortisureIT) in San Francisco, CA on January 5, 2011. His two daughters Priyal and Meghna are the co-founders. Since the beginning, the name Fortisure has represented our commitment to clients, employees and the community. Over the years we have participated in start-ups and supported meaningful causes. Foremost in our name is the importance of “fortitude”. It is derived from the Latin word, fortis, meaning strength, courage, and bravery even during challenging times or in the face of adversity. To facilitate this confidence, one needs the comfort of “assurance” and support for the “future”. So Fortisure was created based on the notion that we would provide companies, partners and their employees with the strength, courage, bravery and support they need for the future. Today, FortisureIT (pronounced fort-i-sure-I.T.) reflects that same commitment. This time, it is a commitment to our clients nationally, to our employees, to the Akron-Canton community and to our nation at large.
